# Underwater Level — Unimplemented Ideas & Variations

This document tracks ideas from the initial brainstorm that were not included in the primary overhaul to keep the focus on the "Little Mermaid" / Sea Witch core loop.

## Mechanics & Hazards

1. **Currents — Forced Movement**
   - Add **current nodes** that push the player in a direction.
   - Puts a "pin" in it — could be added later for more spatial routing.

2. **Crab Thieves — Inventory Hazard**
   - Crabs that swipe items from your inventory.
   - Deemed too random/harmless for the "vibe" right now.

3. **Buried Items — Multi-Step Unlocks**
   - Requiring one item to open a clam and another to get the reward.
   - Initially simplified to stay with the standard interaction model.

4. **Deepwater Pressure — Slot Reduction**
   - Below a certain depth, inventory capacity drops from 4 to 3.
   - Could be implemented later with diving gear as a counter.

5. **Seahorse Escort**
   - A companion that follows you and is needed for certain interactions.
   - Rejected to keep the inventory logic clean.

## UI & Visuals

1. **Gatekeeper Clams as Zone Barriers**
   - Using clams as standard "locked door" nodes.
   - Preferred shipwreck or other thematic barriers instead of just static nodes.

2. **Refined Mermaid Interactions**
   - More complex mermaid "life" (traveling in groups, reactions to player).
   - Currently settled on rotating/colored mermaids with circular paths.
